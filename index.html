Compliance Checklist

The 2026 CEMS Compliance Audit: 5 Points You Cannot Ignore

Environmental compliance is no longer a paperwork exercise; it is a digital mandate. State Boards like MPCB and GPCB are moving toward automated AI-driven audits. Here is your checklist for 2026:

1. The 95% Uptime Rule

If your data transmission drops below 95% for a month, expect a digital notice. ODAMS prevents this with 30-day local buffering.

2. 15-Minute Sync Windows

Real-time data must reach the central portal within 15 minutes. High-latency systems are being flagged for inspection.

3. Tamper-Proof Encryption

Raw data from analyzers must be signed and encrypted before cloud transit to ensure integrity during SPCB audits.

4. Multi-Cloud Redundancy

Relying on a single server is a risk. We utilize AWS and Oracle Cloud to ensure your compliance records are never lost.
